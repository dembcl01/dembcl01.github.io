What I did this summer

This summer I was in Decorah doing research with Dr. McNicoll in the Biology department. I was studying Bumblebees in the tall grass prairie. My main research question is "is there a correlation between the flowers avaliable to a bee and the abundance and diversity of bees in the community?" My hypothesis is that prairies with more diverse flowering plant species will have a more diverse community of bees. I am still in the middle of processing my data, so we will see.
